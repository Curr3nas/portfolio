Hello! My name is Douglas, and I am an aspiring full stack developer.

I am currently a Web Development student in Thinkful's Software Engineering Immersion program. I love coding because I enjoy the rush of being able to solve puzzles and everything about code for me is just solving a puzzle! I am particularly fond of making the functionality of a site work smoothly and seamlessly.

I first became interested in web development when I was put in charge of maintaining the used motorcycle inventory page of the Harley-Davidson dealership where I used to work. I decided to follow through on that interest when I realized that I could make a better website for my church.

If I am not working on an app or a site, then I am usually gaming. I also serve at my Church and am a voracious reader. My current pursuits include getting my CompTIA A+ certification and learning how to speak Chinese.
